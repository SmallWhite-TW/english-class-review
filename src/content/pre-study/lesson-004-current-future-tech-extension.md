# Lesson 004 Extension: Real Future Tech in 2024-2026

**Date:** 2026-04-09  
**Purpose:** Connect the old videos with real current technology  
**Format:** English-Chinese Parallel Notes

---

## Why This Extension? (為什麼要補這份延伸教材？)

- **The two lesson videos feel dated because many of their "future" ideas come from an earlier stage of technology.**  
  這兩支課程影片會讓人覺得有年代感，因為它們所說的「未來」其實來自比較早期的科技想像。

- **This extension helps you talk about what is actually happening now, especially in 2024, 2025, and 2026.**  
  這份延伸教材可以幫你把話題拉回現在，特別是 2024、2025、2026 真正正在發生的科技進展。

- **A useful class angle is: "The videos are old, but some of their ideas are becoming real now."**  
  一個很好用的上課角度是：「影片雖然舊，但裡面有些想像現在真的正在變成現實。」

---

## Part 1: Robots Are Becoming More General-Purpose

### 1. What is happening now? (現在發生了什麼？)

- **Boston Dynamics says its new electric Atlas is part of its journey toward a commercial humanoid robot, and it is already being field-tested with Hyundai.**  
  Boston Dynamics 表示，他們全新的電動 Atlas 是邁向商業化人形機器人的一部分，而且已經開始和 Hyundai 進行實地測試。

- **This means robots are moving from lab demos to real industrial workflows.**  
  這代表機器人正從實驗室展示，逐步走向真實工業流程。

- **NVIDIA published GR00T N1 on March 17, 2025, an open foundation model for humanoid robots that can follow language instructions and perform household-style tasks.**  
  NVIDIA 在 2025 年 3 月 17 日發布了 GR00T N1，這是一個開放權重的人形機器人基礎模型，可以理解語言指令，並執行偏家庭情境的任務。

### 2. Why it matters (為什麼重要？)

- **Old videos showed special robots for special jobs. Now the goal is more flexible robots that can learn many tasks.**  
  舊影片裡的機器人多半是「為特定工作而設計」；現在的方向則是發展能學會多種任務、更有彈性的機器人。

- **The big shift is from "a machine for one purpose" to "a robot that can adapt."**  
  最大的轉變，是從「單一用途機器」走向「可以適應不同情境的機器人」。

### 3. Useful English (好用英文)

- **Humanoid robots are moving closer to real-world deployment.**  
  人形機器人正逐步接近真實世界部署。

- **The key difference now is flexibility, not just mechanical ability.**  
  現在最大的差別在於彈性，而不只是機械能力。

- **Robots are starting to learn from language, video, and real-world data.**  
  機器人開始從語言、影像，以及真實世界資料中學習。

## Part 2: Smart Homes Are Becoming Open Systems

### 1. What is happening now? (現在發生了什麼？)

- **The Matter smart home standard has been expanding quickly. In the Matter 1.3 release, announced by the Connectivity Standards Alliance, support was added for energy reporting, EV charging equipment, leak detectors, water valves, ovens, cooktops, dryers, and more.**  
  Matter 智慧家庭標準正在快速擴張。Connectivity Standards Alliance 公布的 Matter 1.3 版本，已加入能源回報、電動車充電設備、漏水感測器、水閥、烤箱、爐台、烘衣機等支援。

- **Home Assistant announced on March 10, 2025 that its Matter implementation had been officially certified.**  
  Home Assistant 在 2025 年 3 月 10 日宣布，它的 Matter 實作已正式通過認證。

- **This shows that the smart home is becoming less about one brand and more about interoperability.**  
  這表示智慧家庭正逐漸從單一品牌生態，走向跨品牌互通。

### 2. Why it matters (為什麼重要？)

- **In old videos, a smart home looked like a futuristic control panel. Today, the bigger idea is whether devices from different brands can work together locally and reliably.**  
  在舊影片裡，智慧家庭看起來像是一個很炫的控制面板；但今天更重要的問題是，不同品牌的設備能不能在本地穩定協作。

- **The future home is not only "smart." It is also connected, interoperable, and more energy-aware.**  
  未來的家不只是「智慧」，還要能連接、互通，而且更懂得管理能源。

### 3. Useful English (好用英文)

- **The modern smart home is built around interoperability.**  
  現代智慧家庭是建立在互通性之上的。

- **Open standards make smart homes more practical and sustainable.**  
  開放標準讓智慧家庭更實用，也更具永續性。

- **Today, a smart home is not just convenient. It is also about privacy, reliability, and energy management.**  
  現在的智慧家庭不只是方便，也關係到隱私、穩定性與能源管理。

## Part 3: Solar Technology Is Getting Real Efficiency Gains

### 1. What is happening now? (現在發生了什麼？)

- **Oxford PV announced on September 5, 2024 that it had started commercial deployment of perovskite-silicon tandem solar panels in the U.S.**  
  Oxford PV 在 2024 年 9 月 5 日宣布，已開始在美國商業部署鈣鈦礦-矽串疊太陽能板。

- **According to the company, these panels can produce up to 20% more energy than standard silicon panels.**  
  根據該公司說法，這些面板的發電量最高可比標準矽面板多出 20%。

- **This is important because future homes and cities need better clean energy systems, not only smarter screens and sensors.**  
  這很重要，因為未來住宅和城市真正需要的，不只是更聰明的螢幕和感測器，而是更好的潔淨能源系統。

### 2. Why it matters (為什麼重要？)

- **The old home video talked about solar-powered devices as an interesting idea. Now next-generation solar is becoming a real commercial technology.**  
  舊的住宅影片把太陽能設備當成一種有趣概念；但現在次世代太陽能已經開始進入真實商業化。

- **This is a good example of a "future technology" that has moved beyond imagination.**  
  這是一個很好的例子，說明某些「未來科技」已經走出想像，進入現實應用。

### 3. Useful English (好用英文)

- **Future technology is not only digital. It also includes better energy systems.**  
  未來科技不只有數位科技，也包括更好的能源系統。

- **Solar innovation is becoming more commercial and more efficient.**  
  太陽能創新正變得更商業化，也更有效率。

- **Clean energy may be one of the most important parts of future living.**  
  潔淨能源可能是未來生活中最重要的一部分。

## Part 4: Houses Themselves Are Changing

### 1. What is happening now? (現在發生了什麼？)

- **ICON announced in May 2025 the completion of 3D-printed homes for Texas A&M student housing.**  
  ICON 在 2025 年 5 月宣布完成德州農工大學學生住宅的 3D 列印房屋。

- **This means the "future home" is not only about appliances inside the house. It is also about how homes are designed and built.**  
  這代表「未來住宅」不只是屋內設備升級，也包括房子本身的設計與建造方式正在改變。

### 2. Why it matters (為什麼重要？)

- **Old future-home videos focused on lifestyle gadgets. Today, people also care about construction speed, sustainability, and housing affordability.**  
  舊的未來住宅影片比較聚焦生活型設備；但現在人們也很關心施工速度、永續性，以及住宅可負擔性。

- **The future of housing is not only smart control. It is also smarter construction.**  
  住宅的未來不只是更聰明的控制系統，也包括更聰明的建造方式。

### 3. Useful English (好用英文)

- **The future home is also about how we build, not only how we live.**  
  未來住宅不只和我們怎麼住有關，也和我們怎麼蓋有關。

- **3D-printed homes show that construction technology is changing too.**  
  3D 列印住宅說明建築技術本身也在改變。

## Part 5: Space Technology Still Represents the Long-Term Future

### 1. What is happening now? (現在發生了什麼？)

- **NASA says Artemis II is the first crewed Artemis flight and a key step toward long-term missions to the Moon and eventually Mars.**  
  NASA 表示，Artemis II 是 Artemis 計畫中的首次載人飛行，也是邁向長期月球與最終火星任務的重要一步。

- **As of 2026, NASA is treating lunar exploration as part of a long-term human exploration roadmap.**  
  到了 2026 年，NASA 已將月球探索視為長期人類太空探索藍圖的一部分。

### 2. Why it matters (為什麼重要？)

- **In the old robot video, space exploration sounded like a distant future application. Today, space exploration is still difficult, but it is part of real mission planning.**  
  在舊的機器人影片裡，太空探索聽起來像很遙遠的未來應用；但今天它雖然仍然困難，卻已經是實際任務規劃的一部分。

- **This is useful if you want to talk about "future technology" in a larger human context.**  
  如果你想從更大的角度談「未來科技」，這會是一個很好用的主題。

### 3. Useful English (好用英文)

- **Space technology is still one of the clearest symbols of the future.**  
  太空科技仍然是最具代表性的未來象徵之一。

- **Current missions are turning long-term ideas into practical steps.**  
  現在的任務正把長期構想一步步轉成實際行動。

## Part 6: Then vs. Now (影片中的未來 vs 現在的未來)

- **Then: smart control panels looked futuristic. Now: interoperability, local control, and energy management matter more.**  
  以前：智慧控制面板看起來很未來。現在：互通性、本地控制與能源管理更重要。

- **Then: robots were shown as impressive machines. Now: people want robots that can learn flexible tasks.**  
  以前：機器人被當成很厲害的機器展示。現在：人們更希望機器人能學習彈性任務。

- **Then: solar power was a cool idea. Now: next-generation solar panels are entering commercial use.**  
  以前：太陽能像是一個很酷的概念。現在：次世代太陽能板已經開始進入商業應用。

- **Then: future homes meant cool gadgets. Now: future homes also mean energy systems, construction methods, and open ecosystems.**  
  以前：未來住宅等於酷炫家電。現在：未來住宅還包含能源系統、建造方法與開放生態。

## Part 7: Discussion Questions (討論問題中英對照)

1. **Which is more important for the future: smarter robots or smarter energy systems? Why?**  
   你覺得未來更重要的是更聰明的機器人，還是更聰明的能源系統？為什麼？

2. **Do you think the biggest future-home change will come from AI, energy technology, or construction technology?**  
   你覺得未來住宅最大的變化，會來自 AI、能源技術，還是建築技術？

3. **Which current technology feels most real to you: humanoid robots, Matter smart homes, advanced solar panels, or 3D-printed houses?**  
   哪一種現在的科技最讓你覺得「真的已經來了」：人形機器人、Matter 智慧家庭、先進太陽能板，還是 3D 列印房屋？

4. **Do old "future" videos still have learning value? Why?**  
   你覺得這種舊的「未來科技」影片還有學習價值嗎？為什麼？

## Part 8: One Easy Speaking Answer (可直接拿去上課的短回答)

**The lesson videos feel a little old, but I think they are still useful. Some ideas in the videos, like smart homes, robots, and solar energy, are becoming more real now. For example, companies are testing humanoid robots in real workplaces, and Matter is making smart homes more practical. I think the biggest difference is that future technology today is less about imagination and more about real-world use.**  
這堂課的影片看起來有點舊，但我覺得它們還是有用。因為影片裡的一些概念，例如智慧住宅、機器人和太陽能，現在正變得更真實。舉例來說，有公司已經在真實工作場域測試人形機器人，而 Matter 也讓智慧家庭變得更實用。我覺得現在和以前最大的差別是，如今的未來科技比較不是停留在想像，而是開始進入真實應用。
