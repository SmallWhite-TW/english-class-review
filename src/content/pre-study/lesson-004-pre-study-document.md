# English Course Pre-study Document (Lesson 004)

**Date:** 2026-04-09  
**Topic:** Innovation, Robotics & Homes of the Future  
**Format:** English-Chinese Parallel Notes

---

## 1-Minute Quick Review (上課前 1 分鐘速讀)

- **British innovation is shown through robotics, space-related technology, high-performance cars, and luxury car manufacturing.**  
  英國的創新科技體現在機器人、太空相關技術、高性能跑車，以及豪華汽車製造上。

- **The snake-arm robot by OC Robotics can enter confined or dangerous spaces where humans cannot easily work.**  
  OC Robotics 的蛇形機器人可以進入人類不容易作業的狹小或危險空間。

- **Homes of the future may use solar energy, rain water, smart control panels, and flexible building design.**  
  未來住宅可能會使用太陽能、雨水回收、智慧控制面板，以及可彈性調整的建築設計。

- **Both videos show how technology can make life safer, more efficient, and more comfortable.**  
  兩支影片都在說明科技如何讓生活更安全、更有效率，也更舒適。

---

## Part 1: Innovation is GREAT - Part 2

**Video:** _Innovation is GREAT - Part 2_

### 1. Key Vocabulary (關鍵字彙對照)

- **Innovation** (n.)  
  創新

- **Cutting edge** (adj. phr.)  
  尖端的、最前沿的

- **Robotised** (adj.)  
  機器人化的、自動化的

- **Confined space** (n. phr.)  
  密閉空間、狹小空間

- **Space exploration** (n. phr.)  
  太空探索

- **High-performance** (adj.)  
  高性能的

- **Low-emissions** (adj.)  
  低排放的

- **Carbon fibre composite** (n. phr.)  
  碳纖維複合材料

- **Craftsmanship** (n.)  
  工藝、職人工藝

- **Lane departure system** (n. phr.)  
  車道偏離警示系統

### 2. Video Summary (影片重點對照)

- **The snake-arm robot is designed to enter narrow or dangerous places, such as areas with radiation, underwater locations, or small industrial spaces.**  
  蛇形機器人被設計用來進入狹窄或危險的地方，例如有輻射的區域、水下環境，或小型工業空間。

- **The video also introduces British innovation in Formula 1 technology, McLaren's low-emissions sports car, and Rolls-Royce's combination of advanced engineering and traditional craftsmanship.**  
  影片也介紹了英國在 F1 科技、McLaren 低排放跑車，以及 Rolls-Royce 將先進工程和傳統工藝結合的創新。

- **A key message is that innovation comes from creativity, problem-solving, and the courage to take on difficult challenges.**  
  影片的重要訊息是：創新來自創意、解決問題的能力，以及面對困難挑戰的勇氣。

### 3. Quick Chinese Understanding (中文快速理解)

git- **This video is not only about one machine. It is about the broader idea of innovation.**  
  這支影片不只是介紹某一台機器，而是在談更大的概念，也就是「創新」。

- **The robot example is useful because it clearly shows how technology can do work that is difficult or dangerous for humans.**  
  機器人的例子很好懂，因為它清楚展示科技如何替人類完成困難或危險的工作。

- **The car examples show that innovation is also connected to design, materials, performance, and user experience.**  
  汽車的例子則說明，創新也和設計、材料、性能，以及使用者體驗有關。

### 4. Short Sharing Version (可直接分享的中英文對照短講)

**One video I watched is about British innovation. It introduces a snake-arm robot that can work in dangerous or confined spaces, which could even be useful for space exploration. The video also shows how British companies use advanced technology in cars like McLaren and Rolls-Royce. I think the most interesting point is that innovation is not only about new machines, but also about creative thinking and practical problem-solving.**  
我看的其中一支影片是在談英國創新。影片介紹了一種蛇形機器人，它可以在危險或狹小空間中工作，甚至可能應用在太空探索。影片也展示了英國企業如何把先進科技應用在 McLaren 和 Rolls-Royce 這類汽車上。我覺得最有趣的地方是，創新不只是新的機器，也包含創意思考與實際解決問題的能力。

### 5. Speaking Support (口說支架對照)

- **One of the videos I watched is about British innovation.**  
  我看的其中一支影片是在談英國創新。

- **What impressed me most is the snake-arm robot.**  
  最吸引我的是蛇形機器人。

- **It can enter dangerous or confined spaces.**  
  它可以進入危險或狹小的空間。

- **I think the most important part of innovation is solving problems.**  
  我覺得創新最重要的地方是解決問題。

### 6. Useful Sentence Patterns (實用句型對照)

- **It is designed to get into confined spaces.**  
  它被設計用來進入狹小空間。

- **One example of innovation is the use of carbon fibre composite in sports cars.**  
  創新的一個例子，是在跑車中使用碳纖維複合材料。

- **What makes it special is the combination of technology and craftsmanship.**  
  讓它特別的地方，是科技與工藝的結合。

## Part 2: Homes of the Future

**Video:** _Homes of the Future_

### 1. Key Vocabulary (關鍵字彙對照)

- **Hi-tech** (adj.)  
  高科技的

- **Solar energy** (n. phr.)  
  太陽能

- **Rain water** (n. phr.)  
  雨水

- **Energy efficient** (adj. phr.)  
  節能的

- **Control panel** (n. phr.)  
  控制面板

- **Fingerprint recognition** (n. phr.)  
  指紋辨識

- **Automatically** (adv.)  
  自動地

- **Renewable energy** (n. phr.)  
  再生能源

- **Eco-house** (n.)  
  環保住宅

- **Self-cleaning** (adj.)  
  自動清潔的

### 2. Video Summary (影片重點對照)

- **The video shows experimental houses that test new ideas for future living, including flexible structures that can expand as people's needs change.**  
  影片展示了一些實驗住宅，用來測試未來生活的新想法，包括可以隨著居住需求改變而擴充的彈性結構。

- **Future homes may use rain water from the roof, solar energy for heating, and other systems that make houses more energy efficient.**  
  未來住宅可能會利用屋頂收集的雨水、以太陽能加熱，並使用其他讓住宅更節能的系統。

- **Smart homes may include fingerprint entry, automatic shutters, and a control panel for temperature and music.**  
  智慧住宅可能包含指紋進出、自動百葉窗，以及控制溫度和音樂的面板。

- **People in the video imagine features such as a self-cleaning house, an eco-house, and even a robot helper.**  
  影片中的人也想像未來住宅可以有自動清潔功能、環保設計，甚至機器人助手。

### 3. Quick Chinese Understanding (中文快速理解)

- **The main idea is that future homes are not only modern in appearance, but also smarter, greener, and more convenient.**  
  這支影片的核心概念是：未來的家不只是外表現代，而是更聰明、更環保，也更方便。

- **This topic is easier to talk about because it is connected to daily life.**  
  這個主題比較容易發揮，因為它和日常生活很接近。

- **You can focus on energy saving, smart control, or comfort when sharing your opinion.**  
  你在分享意見時，可以聚焦在節能、智慧控制，或生活舒適度這三個方向。

### 4. Short Sharing Version (可直接分享的中英文對照短講)

**Another video is about homes of the future. It shows houses that use solar energy, rain water, and smart control systems. Some homes can even change in size when people's lives change. I think this kind of design is interesting because it combines environmental protection with convenience. My favourite idea is a smart home that saves energy and makes daily life easier.**  
另一支影片是在談未來住宅。影片展示了使用太陽能、雨水，以及智慧控制系統的房子。有些房子甚至可以隨著人們生活狀況的改變而調整大小。我覺得這種設計很有趣，因為它把環保和便利結合在一起。我最喜歡的概念，是一種能節能又讓日常生活更輕鬆的智慧住宅。

### 5. Speaking Support (口說支架對照)

- **Another video is about homes of the future.**  
  另一支影片是在談未來住宅。

- **I think the most practical design is the energy-saving system.**  
  我覺得最實用的設計是節能系統。

- **If a house can control the temperature automatically, life will be more convenient.**  
  如果房子能自動控制溫度，生活會更方便。

- **I hope future homes will be more eco-friendly and more comfortable.**  
  我希望未來的家會更環保，也更舒適。

### 6. Useful Sentence Patterns (實用句型對照)

- **In the future, homes may use more renewable energy.**  
  未來住宅可能會使用更多再生能源。

- **I would like a house that can save energy automatically.**  
  我想要一間可以自動節能的房子。

- **This technology makes homes more efficient and comfortable.**  
  這項技術讓住宅更有效率，也更舒適。

## Part 3: Compare the Two Videos (兩支影片比較)

### 1. Common Ideas (共同主題對照)

- **Both videos focus on how technology improves human life.**  
  兩支影片都在談科技如何改善人類生活。

- **Both show that innovation is not only about being impressive, but also about solving real problems.**  
  兩支影片都指出，創新不只是讓人覺得厲害，更重要的是解決真實問題。

- **One video focuses more on industry and machines, while the other focuses more on daily life and living spaces.**  
  一支影片比較偏產業與機械，另一支則比較偏日常生活與居住空間。

### 2. Comparison Sentences (比較句型對照)

- **The first video focuses more on industrial and engineering innovation, while the second video focuses more on home life.**  
  第一支影片比較聚焦在工業與工程創新，第二支則更偏向居家生活。

- **Both videos show advanced technology, but they apply it in different areas.**  
  兩支影片都展示了先進科技，只是應用領域不同。

- **I think home technology is more practical for daily life, while robotics technology is more exciting and impressive.**  
  我覺得居家科技對日常生活更實用，而機器人科技則更讓人覺得新奇又厲害。

### 3. Easy Comparison Logic (簡單比較邏輯)

- **Both videos are about future technology.**  
  兩支影片都和未來科技有關。

- **The first one is more about work and industry. The second one is more about family life and daily living.**  
  第一支比較偏工作與產業，第二支比較偏家庭生活與日常居住。

- **Personally, I prefer ... because ...**  
  就我個人來說，我比較喜歡……因為……

## Part 4: Discussion Questions (討論問題中英對照)

1. **Which innovation in the first video impressed you most: robotics, sports car technology, or luxury car design? Why?**  
   第一支影片中，最讓你印象深刻的創新是什麼：機器人、跑車科技，還是豪華汽車設計？為什麼？

2. **Do you think robots should do more dangerous work instead of humans? Why or why not?**  
   你覺得機器人應該代替人類去做更多危險工作嗎？為什麼？

3. **Which future home technology would be most useful in your daily life?**  
   哪一種未來住宅科技最有可能對你的日常生活有幫助？

4. **Would you prefer a traditional home or a smart eco-house? Why?**  
   你會比較喜歡傳統住宅還是智慧環保住宅？為什麼？

5. **Do you think advanced technology always makes life better, or can it create new problems?**  
   你覺得先進科技總是讓生活更好嗎，還是也可能帶來新的問題？

6. **Which video do you think is easier to discuss in English? Why?**  
   你覺得哪一支影片比較容易用英文討論？為什麼？

### Answer Hints (答題提示對照)

- **Robots can protect workers from dangerous jobs.**  
  機器人可以保護工人，不用親自從事危險工作。

- **Smart home systems are practical because they save time and energy.**  
  智慧家庭系統很實用，因為它們可以節省時間和能源。

- **Advanced technology is helpful, but it can also make people too dependent on machines.**  
  先進科技很有幫助，但也可能讓人太依賴機器。

## Part 5: Speaking Practice (口說預演中英對照)

### 1. 30-Second Opinion Starter

**I watched two videos about future technology. One is about innovation in robotics and cars, and the other is about smart homes. I think both videos are interesting because they show how technology can improve safety, efficiency, and comfort. Personally, I am more interested in ... because ...**  
我看了兩支和未來科技有關的影片。一支是在談機器人和汽車創新，另一支是在談智慧住宅。我覺得這兩支影片都很有趣，因為它們展現科技如何提升安全、效率和舒適度。就我個人來說，我對……比較有興趣，因為……

### 2. Personal Answer Template

- **The most interesting idea in the lesson is ...**  
  這堂課最有趣的概念是……

- **I think this technology is useful because ...**  
  我覺得這項技術很有用，因為……

- **In the future, I hope ...**  
  在未來，我希望……

- **One possible problem is ...**  
  一個可能的問題是……

### 3. Mini Translation Practice (中翻英迷你練習)

- **I am most interested in future homes.**  
  我最有興趣的是未來住宅。

- **I think smart homes are closer to my daily life.**  
  我覺得智慧家庭比較貼近我的日常生活。

- **Robots can help people do dangerous work.**  
  機器人可以幫助人類做危險工作。

- **Technology should make life safer and more convenient.**  
  科技應該讓生活更安全，也更方便。

## Part 6: Learning Goals (預習目標中英對照)

1. **Be able to explain these words in simple English: innovation, confined space, carbon fibre composite, energy efficient, renewable energy, and fingerprint recognition.**  
   能用簡單英文解釋這些字：innovation、confined space、carbon fibre composite、energy efficient、renewable energy、fingerprint recognition。

2. **Practice giving your opinion with: "I think this innovation is useful because ...", "What impressed me most was ...", and "In the future, I would like ..."**  
   練習用這些句型表達自己的意見："I think this innovation is useful because ..."、"What impressed me most was ..."、"In the future, I would like ..."

3. **Be ready to compare industrial innovation and smart home technology in simple English.**  
   練習用簡單英文比較產業創新與智慧住宅科技。

4. **Practice saying innovation, exploration, efficient, recognition, and craftsmanship clearly.**  
   練習清楚唸出 innovation、exploration、efficient、recognition、craftsmanship。

### Final Pre-class Tips (上課前最後提醒)

- **If you do not have much time, remember at least three key words for each video.**  
  如果你時間不多，至少先記住每支影片三個關鍵字。

- **Think of your opinion in Chinese first, then turn it into one or two simple English sentences.**  
  先用中文想好自己的看法，再把它轉成一到兩句簡單英文。

- **You do not need to speak for a long time. It is enough if you understand the topic and can express two or three ideas clearly.**  
  你不需要講很長，只要聽得懂主題，並清楚講出兩到三個想法就夠了。
