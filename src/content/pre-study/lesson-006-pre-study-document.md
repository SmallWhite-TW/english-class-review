### 第五堂先複習的 6 個高頻重點

- **`stroll` means a slow, relaxing walk.**  
  `stroll` 是悠閒、放鬆的散步。

- **`fun` and `funny` are different. A class can be fun, but a joke is funny.**  
  `fun` 和 `funny` 不一樣。課程可以是 `fun`，笑話才通常是 `funny`。

- **`lose track of time` is more natural than `forget the time` in many daily situations.**  
  在很多生活情境裡，`lose track of time` 比 `forget the time` 更自然。

- **`work overtime` describes longer working hours, while `overworked` describes a tired condition.**  
  `work overtime` 偏向加班這件事，`overworked` 偏向工作過量後的狀態。

- **`snorkelling` and `scuba-diving` should not be mixed up.**  
  `snorkelling` 和 `scuba-diving` 不要混用。

- **`double-edged sword`, `upside`, and `downside` are especially useful for this PDF topic.**  
  `double-edged sword`、`upside`、`downside` 這三個詞，對這份教材特別實用。

## Part 1: What Lesson 006 Should Add Beyond Lesson 005

### 1. New Speaking Goal (這堂更需要練的能力)

- **Lesson 005 focused more on understanding and vocabulary building.**  
  第五堂比較偏理解教材與建立字彙。

- **Lesson 006 should focus more on answering discussion questions in full sentences.**  
  第六堂更應該把重心放在用完整句回答討論題。

- **Students should try to move from keywords to complete opinions with reasons.**  
  學生要練習從關鍵字式回答，往「完整意見加理由」移動。

### 2. Recommended Answer Structure (建議回答骨架)

- **Point:** state the invention, product, or idea.  
  `Point`：先講要談的發明、產品或概念。

- **Reason:** explain why it is useful, impressive, or problematic.  
  `Reason`：說明為什麼它有用、令人印象深刻，或有問題。

- **Example:** connect it to work, life, or society.  
  `Example`：把它連回工作、生活，或社會情境。

可直接套用：

**I think ___ is one of the most useful innovations because ___. For example, ___. However, one possible downside is ___.**  
我認為 ___ 是最有用的創新之一，因為 ___。例如，___。不過，一個可能的缺點是 ___。

## Part 3: Likely Discussion Questions and Stronger Answers

### 1. Which innovation is the most valuable for humanity?

**I think the internet is one of the most valuable innovations for humanity because it completely changed how people learn, work, and communicate. It gives people fast access to information and allows global collaboration. However, it also creates problems like misinformation and overdependence.**  
我認為網際網路是對人類最有價值的創新之一，因為它徹底改變了人們學習、工作與溝通的方式。它讓人們能快速取得資訊，也能進行全球協作。不過，它也帶來假資訊和過度依賴等問題。

### 2. Which innovation may be destructive?

**I think some technologies can be destructive when people use them without enough responsibility. For example, nuclear technology can provide power, but it can also be dangerous if safety is ignored.**  
我認為有些科技如果在人們缺乏足夠責任感的情況下使用，可能會帶來破壞性。比如核能技術可以提供能源，但如果忽略安全，也可能很危險。

### 3. What kind of invention is still urgently needed?

**One urgently needed invention is better medical or caregiving technology for aging societies. It would be helpful if smart devices could support older people more safely at home.**  
一個仍然急需的發明，是能因應高齡社會的更好醫療或照護科技。如果智慧裝置可以更安全地支援長者居家生活，會非常有幫助。

### 4. Would students like to live in a smart home?

**I would like to live in a smart home because it could save time and energy. For example, I like the idea of automatic temperature control and energy-efficient systems. But I would still worry about privacy, cost, and technical problems.**  
學生可能會想住在智慧住宅裡，因為它可以節省時間和能源。例如，自動溫度控制和節能系統就是很吸引人的功能。不過，隱私、成本和故障問題仍然值得擔心。

## Part 6: This Time, Focus on These Corrections (這次特別注意的表達修正)

- **Do not answer with only a noun or a short phrase.**  
  不要只用名詞或超短片語作答。

- **Try not to stop after saying a keyword like `privacy` or `convenient`; add one reason.**  
  不要講完 `privacy` 或 `convenient` 就停住，後面要再補一個理由。

- **When comparing advantages and disadvantages, use linking words such as `because`, `but`, `however`, and `for example`.**  
  在比較優缺點時，要刻意使用 `because`、`but`、`however`、`for example` 這些連接語。

- **When giving opinions, use complete patterns such as `I think... because...` instead of isolated vocabulary.**  
  表達意見時，要用 `I think... because...` 這類完整句型，而不是零散單字。

## One Short Version Students Can Say in Class

**This material is about innovation and future living. I think the most interesting idea is that technology can solve difficult problems and also make daily life more convenient. For example, robots can work in dangerous places, and smart homes can save energy and time. However, technology is a double-edged sword, so we should also think about privacy, cost, and safety.**  
這份教材是在談創新與未來生活。學生會覺得最有趣的地方，是科技既能解決困難問題，也能讓日常生活更方便。比如機器人可以在危險場所工作，而智慧住宅可以節省能源和時間。不過，科技是一把雙面刃，所以也要思考隱私、成本與安全問題。
