# English Course Pre-study Document (Lesson 002)

**Date:** 2026-03-24
**Topic:** Taiwan Economic Trends, Pop Culture & International News

---

## Part 1: Business & Economy

**Article:** _Taiwan's industrial, manufacturing output hits record high in February_

### 1. Key Vocabulary (關鍵字彙)

- **Industrial output** (n.) 工業產值
- **Manufacturing activity** (n.) 製造業活動
- **Record high** (n. phr.) 歷史新高
- **Year-on-year (YoY)** (adj./adv.) 與去年同期相比
- **Surging** (adj.) 激增的、飆升的
- **Infrastructure** (n.) 基礎設施
- **High-performance computing (HPC)** (n.) 高效能運算
- **Semiconductor** (n.) 半導體
- **Hampered** (v.) 阻礙、妨礙

### 2. Article Summary (文章要點)

- **Growth Driver:** The record-breaking performance in February was primarily driven by the global demand for **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** applications.
- **Key Sectors:** Significant growth was seen in electronic components, integrated circuits (ICs), and optical products.
- **Divergence:** While AI-related tech sectors are booming, traditional industries remain relatively weak, except for the machinery sector which supports semiconductor manufacturing.

## Part 2: Culture & Entertainment

**Article:** _Tens of thousands attend One Piece parade in Kaohsiung_

### 1. Key Vocabulary (關鍵字彙)

- **Streaming giant** (n. phr.) 串流巨頭 (指 Netflix)
- **Parade** (n.) 遊行
- **Costume** (n.) 裝扮、戲服
- **Worldwide phenomenon** (n. phr.) 全球性現象
- **Cosplay** (n./v.) 角色扮演
- **Live-action version** (n. phr.) 真人版
- **Symbolize** (v.) 象徵
- **Safe voyages** (n. phr.) 航行平安

### 2. Article Summary (文章要點)

- **Event Purpose:** Netflix organized a massive parade in Kaohsiung to celebrate the second season of the **One Piece** live-action series.
- **Scale:** Tens of thousands of fans from all over Taiwan (including central and northern regions) gathered in Kaohsiung, many dressed as their favorite characters like Luffy and Chopper.
- **Highlight:** The parade featured a model of the "Going Merry" ship and started with a blessing from Taichung's Jenn Lann Temple for "safe voyages."

## Part 3: International Relations & Diplomacy

**Video:** _Foreign Ministry confronts Denmark for labeling Taiwanese nationals as Chinese_

### 1. Key Vocabulary (關鍵字彙)

- **Confront** (v.) 正面交鋒、對質
- **National** (n.) 國民、公民
- **Discriminatory** (adj.) 歧視性的
- **Diplomatic channel** (n. phr.) 外交管道

### 2. Article Summary (文章要點)

- **The Issue:** Taiwan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA) is protesting against Denmark for labeling Taiwanese citizens as "Chinese" on their residency permits.
- **Taiwan's Stance:** MOFA is using diplomatic channels to urge the Danish government to respect Taiwanese identity and correct the "discriminatory" labeling.

## Part 4: Transportation & Logistics

**Video:** _Airlines consider cutting flights to Asia amid uncertain fuel supplies_

### 1. Key Vocabulary (關鍵字彙)

- **Fuel supply** (n. phr.) 燃料供應
- **Uncertainty** (n.) 不確定性
- **Scale back** (v. phr.) 縮減、縮減規模
- **Operational costs** (n. phr.) 營運成本

### 2. Article Summary (文章要點)

- **Current Situation:** International airlines are concerned about the stability of aviation fuel supplies in certain Asian regions.
- **Impact:** This uncertainty might lead to a "scaling back" of flight frequencies to manage operational costs and avoid disruptions.

## Part 5: Discussion Questions (討論問題)

1. **Economy:** Do you think Taiwan's heavy reliance on the semiconductor and AI industries is a strength or a potential risk?
2. **Culture:** What is your opinion on live-action adaptations of popular anime? Do they usually meet fans' expectations?
3. **Diplomacy:** How should Taiwan handle being mislabeled in international documents? Does it affect your daily life or travel experience?
4. **Travel:** If airlines reduce flights to Asia, how would it affect your future travel plans or business logistics?

---

## Part 6: Learning Goals (預習目標)

1. **Pronunciation:** Practice saying "Artificial Intelligence," "Semiconductor," "Phenomenon," and "Discriminatory."
2. **Sentence Pattern:** Try to use the phrase **"driven by..."** (由...所驅動/帶動) and **"scaling back"** (縮減規模).
   - _Example 1: "The growth was driven by the global demand for AI."_
   - _Example 2: "Airlines are scaling back their operations due to fuel costs."_
