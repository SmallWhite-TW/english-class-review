# 🎓 English Lesson 001: Learning Dashboard

## 📝 Section 1: Self-Introduction Script
> *This is your speaking draft for classroom interaction.*

Hi everyone, I'm Dave. I'm from Taiwan and I live in Sanchong, New Taipei City.

I work as a **Product Manager**, where I work closely with engineers and other team members to **organize requirements** and **move projects forward**. Recently, I've become very interested in exploring **AI tools** and finding ways to **improve my workflow**.

In my free time, I enjoy learning new things, reading, and traveling. I joined this class because I want to improve my English fluency and gain more confidence through practice. Currently, I usually learn by reading articles and listening to an English podcast called **"Eat Your Crust."**

Nice to meet you all!

## 📚 Section 2: J.B. Klich Vocabulary Notes (Enhanced)

### 🏅 Sports & Leisure (運動與休閒)
*   **Judoka** (n.) — *柔道練習者*
    *   💡 **Tip:** Say "I'm a judoka" or "I train in judo."
*   **Field** (n.) — *運動場；領域*
    *   💡 **Tip:** Use "field" for grass (soccer), "court" for hard floors (basketball).
*   **Hobby** (n.) — *嗜好*
    *   💡 **Tip:** Native speakers often ask "**What do you do for fun?**" instead.
*   **Competitive** (adj.) — *競爭的*
    *   🔗 **Phrase:** **Competitive edge** (競爭優勢).

### 🚗 Life & Rules (生活、交通與規則)
*   **Red-eye** (n.) — *紅眼航班*
    *   💡 **Tip:** Use with verbs like **catch** or **take**.
*   **Deposit** (n./v.) — *押金；存款*
    *   🔗 **Phrase:** **Put down a deposit** (付押金).
*   **Lenient** (adj.) — *寬容的 (規則/處罰)*
*   **Pull over** (v. phr.) — *靠邊停車*

### 🌏 Society & Culture (社會、文化與宗教)
*   **Discrimination** (n.) — *歧視*
    *   🔗 **Phrase:** **Face/Experience discrimination** (遭遇歧視).
*   **Status quo** (n.) — *現狀*
    *   🔗 **Phrase:** **Challenge the status quo** (挑戰現狀).

### 🎬 Movie & Book Genres (電影與小說類型)
*   **Action** (n.) — *動作片*
*   **Sci-Fi** (n.) — *科幻片 (Science Fiction 的縮寫)*
    *   💡 **Tip:** Native speakers almost always use "Sci-Fi."
*   **Rom-com** (n.) — *浪漫喜劇 (Romantic Comedy 的縮寫)*
    *   💡 **Tip:** A very common casual term for lighthearted love stories.
*   **Horror** vs. **Thriller** (n.)
    *   🔍 **Nuance:** **Horror** aims to scare (ghosts, gore). **Thriller** aims to create suspense and tension.
*   **Animation** (n.) — *動畫*
    *   💡 **Tip:** Also called an "Animated feature."
*   **Documentary** (n.) — *紀錄片*
    *   💡 **Tip:** Often shortened to "**Doc**" in conversation.
*   **Mystery** (n.) — *推理/懸疑*
    *   🔗 **Phrase:** **Plot twist** (劇情大反轉).
*   **Fantasy** (n.) — *奇幻/魔幻*
    *   📖 **Ex:** "The Lord of the Rings is a masterpiece of the **fantasy** genre."
*   **Biography** vs. **Memoir** (n.)
    *   🔍 **Nuance:** **Biography** covers a person's entire life. **Memoir** focuses on a specific period or theme.
*   **Non-fiction** (n.) — *非虛構/紀實類*
    *   📖 **Ex:** "I enjoy reading **non-fiction** books about AI and productivity."

### 🍽️ Others (飲食與其他)
*   **Mild** (adj.) — *味淡的；溫和的*
    *   💡 **Tip:** Use this to order non-spicy food: "I'd like it **mild**."
*   **Cooperate** vs. **Collaborate** (v.) — *合作*
    *   🔍 **Nuance:** **Cooperate** = working together (following rules). **Collaborate** = deep intellectual/creative teamwork.

### 📰 Media & Current Events (時事與媒體)
*   **Current affairs** (n.) — *時事新聞*
    *   🔗 **Phrase:** **Keep up with current affairs** (跟進時事).
*   **Silicon shield** (n.) — *矽盾* (Taiwan's semiconductor protection).
